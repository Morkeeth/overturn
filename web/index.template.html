The gap no other feed can express

To settle a market that later gets corrected, a data feed's schema needs two things: an as-of time distinct from when it was published, and a link from a record to the one that supersedes it. We checked nine on-chain settlement systems. Every waiting period in all of them guards against a liar, not against the truth changing.

TxLINE ships both — Confirmed:false → action_discarded, with a batch window that is not the publish time. That is the primitive the settlement layer was missing.
