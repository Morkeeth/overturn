Bet on the World Cup. Settled by a proof, not a bookmaker.

Real markets on real upcoming matches. Someone has staked one side; you take the other with your own wallet. How a bet works

1 · Take

Pick a side

Someone staked YES. You take the other side and match their stake. The pot is now locked on-chain.

2 · Play

The match happens

Nobody can touch the money. Not us, not you, not the person on the other side.

3 · Prove

Settle from a proof

At full time, anyone hands the escrow a TxLINE Merkle proof. It settles only on that, never on the stream.

4 · Collect

The winner is paid

The correct side claims the pot. On a goal that was later overturned, this escrow refuses. That is the point.

Devnet only. These are demonstration markets with play-money SOL, opened to show the settlement engine end to end on fixtures that have not been played yet. The same program is live on mainnet at the same address; putting real money on an open internet market is a different decision than we make for a hackathon.
