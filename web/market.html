Watch it settle — no wallet, no install

Overturn is the settlement layer, not a betting app — it decides, from an on-chain proof, who a sports market pays, and refuses to pay on a goal that didn't happen. Watch the whole thing, one step at a time: a real proof from the wrong match refused, the honest proof settled, the right side paid. The terminal is a replay — the chain is not; every hash it prints is a live mainnet transaction you can open.

Try to break it yourself

There is a live prop on devnet with 0.1 SOL locked in it. Settling is permissionless, so you are allowed to try to settle it, and so is everyone else. Below is a real, valid, unforged TxLINE proof from the other semi-final. It clears the finality guard and the predicate guard. Sign it with your own wallet and watch the escrow refuse it.

We supply the proof, because that needs our TxLINE subscription. You supply the signature, because that needs your wallet. We never see your key and cannot move your money. It costs a devnet transaction fee, which is nothing, and it will fail, which is the point.

Needs a wallet (Phantom / Solflare) set to devnet, with a little devnet SOL for the fee — get some free here. No wallet, or on mainnet? The no-wallet replay at the top of the page needs neither.
